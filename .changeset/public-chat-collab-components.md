Expose focused public client subpaths for custom chat, composer, conversation, and collaboration UI composition.

Adds `@agent-native/core/client/chat`, `@agent-native/core/client/composer`, `@agent-native/core/client/conversation`, and `@agent-native/core/client/collab`, promotes the low-level `TiptapComposer` props/types through the composer surface, exports `dedupeCollabUsersByEmail`, and documents how to rebuild agent chat/sidebar and realtime presence experiences from public pieces.
